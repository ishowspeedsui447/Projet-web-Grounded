Survol de Grounded

Personnages

Réduits à la taille d’un insecte, perdus dans un jardin devenu sauvage et dangereux, quatre ados tentent de comprendre ce qui leur est arrivé. Entre la peur, la débrouille et l’espoir de retrouver leur taille normale, chacun apporte sa personnalité, ses forces et ses faiblesses. Avant de plonger dans leurs histoires, découvre ceux qui forment le cœur de l’aventure Grounded.

Personal Trivia

  • He dreams of becoming famous.
  • Max is allergic to bees.
  • Despite poking fun at Pete, Max is a self-described nerd, specifically mentioning his collection of baseball cards and comic books.
  • He seems to take his collecting hobby somewhat seriously, correcting Pete when he describes a Yoked Girth figure set as "toys" that they're actually "poseable, collectible action figures". He also mentions to Hoops that the reason her own baseball cards degraded might have been due to her not storing them in a "temperature and humidity controlled environment". Max presumably does not perform very well academically, mentioning that the Moldorc Castle Playset "looks like his mom's face after she saw his last report card".
  • Max hates Mosquitoes.
